== Description ==

Profily is a multipurpose, block-based Full Site Editing (FSE) theme, making it incredibly flexible for all types of websites. 
With its global styles, users can effortlessly switch between their favorite color schemes within seconds to match their brand’s identity. The theme also comes pre-designed with over 10+ free ready-to-use patterns for different homepage sections, 
including banners, hero sections, pricing tables, about us, team showcases, testimonials, portfolios, Call to Action elements, Recent Posts, Stats Counters, and more. 
Profily is perfect choice for niche,portfolio , corporate businesses, agencies,  personal brands, creative agencies, freelancers, portfolios, and any other type of business website you can imagine.

== Installation ==

1. Go to Themes > Add New, search for "Gutenly" and then click "Install".
2. After installaion is done click on "Activate".
3. Locate Gutenly Plugin installation notice at the top or visit the Plugins tab from the left sidebar.
4. Then click "Install Now" to install Required Plugins (includes essential theme features)
5. Enjoy!
